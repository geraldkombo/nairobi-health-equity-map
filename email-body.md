# From Data to Demand: A Community-Owned Platform for Health Equity Monitoring Across Kenya's 47 Counties

*Gerald Kombo - Independent Researcher and Developer, Kenya Health Equity Map*

## Sub-theme 2: Digital Health and Evidence Generation Through Community-Led Monitoring

**Introduction:** Community-led monitoring fails when evidence is locked away from the people who need it. Kenya's national statistics often hide local crises. The Kenya Health Equity Map places the key directly in citizens' hands, translating hidden data into a simple Priority Gap Score (0-100) that communities own and use.

**Description of intervention:** This offline digital tool eliminates all gatekeepers. It requires no permission, no login, no data collection, and no internet after a single load. A community health worker opens it on a basic phone in airplane mode and instantly views a one-page evidence brief showing exactly where their local health system is failing. The score itself is a transparent calculation combining travel time, clinic crowding, and poverty.

**Findings and lessons learned:** The data belongs to the people. Turkana scores 92 out of 100 - a severe gap where a mother travels 90 minutes to the nearest clinic, 47% of births happen at home, and 42.6% of households live in poverty. Nairobi scores 40, with 618 mapped clinics and 16.5% poverty. This 52-point gap means one woman travels 90 minutes for basic care while another walks minutes. Communities in Turkana, Tana River (score 89, 72.5% poverty), and Elgeyo-Marakwet ground-truthed these realities and added missing local clinics to OpenStreetMap themselves.

**Conclusion and next steps:** True ownership means direct accountability. Armed with this offline evidence, citizens can walk into legally mandated County Health Management Team meetings (Kenya Health Act 2017) and demand answers: "Last year Turkana scored 92 out of 100, meaning mothers faced 90-minute journeys to clinics. What has changed since then?" No intermediaries. No NGO. Just citizens with undeniable data that cost nothing to produce and belongs to no one but them.
